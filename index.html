Congratulations to Abel Jordan on receiving his third career MPSF Men's Athlete of the Week award!

Jordan clinched first place finishes at the Silver State Invitational in the men's 60m hurdles final and men's 60m dash final.

The Madrid, Spain native ran a 6.65 in the 60m dash to hold the second fastest time among all Division I runners so far this season behind Penn State's Ajani Dwyer (6.55).

He clocked a 7.86 in the men's 60m hurdles final after taking first in the preliminary round with a time of 7.82.

Egonu has been a key member of the Italian national team for almost a decade, including the European Championship in 2021 and the Nations League in 2022. She also had the honour of being one of Italy flagbearers in the Opening Ceremony at Tokyo 2020. Her success has not insulated her from discrimination, and she quit the national team after receiving a torrent of racist abuse on social media following Italy's 2022 World Championship semi-final exit to Brazil. Egonu later reversed that decision, returning to the team in August 2023, but her bold actions sparked a debate in Italy over the issue of race in the country.
